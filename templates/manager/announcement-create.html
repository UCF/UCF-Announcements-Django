New Announcements Features

Announcement Editing

Announcements can now be edited after they've been posted. By navigating to the Manager link at the top of the page, you can view announcements you've posted. Currently, announcements can only be edited by the original creator of the announcement.

Status Check

The announcements on the Manager page are organized by their current approval status. When an announcement is first posted, it will be placed in the "Pending Announcements" tab. Once it has been approved is will be moved into the "Published Announcements" tab.
